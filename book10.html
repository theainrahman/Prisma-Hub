BOOK 10

Fundamentals of managing reference collections

Singer, Carol A.

📅 Publication Year 2012
🏢 Publisher Chicago : Facet Publishing
📚 Call Number Z711 .S57 2012
📓 ISBN 9781856048316
🏷 Abstract This practical manual addresses the continuous cycle of building, evaluating, and maintaining a library's reference collection, regardless of its size. It provides actionable strategies for managing a hybrid collection comprised of traditional print materials, subscription databases, and open-access electronic tools. Singer offers frameworks for drafting reference collection development policies, evaluating vendor packages, allocating budgets, and handling the sensitive process of weeding out obsolete resource records.

DESCRIPTION

Fundamentals of Managing Reference Collections offers insight and best practice for designing, organizing and maintaining both tangible and virtual reference collections, no matter the size, and shows why managing without a plan is a recipe for clutter and confusion.
