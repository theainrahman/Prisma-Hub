Frequently Asked Questions

Click on any question below to read the answer.

What is this website about? +
This website is a digital collection showcase of ten Information Management books available at Perpustakaan Sultan Badlishah (PSB), UiTM. It was developed to help students and researchers discover relevant academic resources.
How many books are featured on this website? +
This website features exactly 10 Information Management books carefully selected from the PSB Library collection. Each book has its own dedicated page with detailed information including title, author, publisher, call number, ISBN, and abstract.
Can I borrow these books from the library? +
Yes! All books featured on this website are available at Perpustakaan Sultan Badlishah (PSB), UiTM Kedah. You can visit the library in person or use the PTAR Portal to check availability and place a reservation.
How do I access the PTAR Portal? +
You can access the PTAR Portal directly at permata.uitm.edu.my. The portal allows UiTM students and staff to search the library catalogue, check book availability, renew loans, and access online databases.
What is Information Management? +
Information Management refers to the collection, organization, maintenance, retrieval, and distribution of information within organizations. It involves strategies, systems, and processes that ensure the right information reaches the right people at the right time to support decision-making and organizational goals.
What browser should I use to view this website? +
This website is best viewed on Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, or Microsoft Edge at a minimum screen resolution of 1366 x 768 pixels. Some features may not display correctly on older browsers.
How do I give feedback about this website? +
We would love to hear from you! Please visit our Feedback page under the support section to share your thoughts, rate your experience, and help us improve. You can also reach us through the Contact Us page.
